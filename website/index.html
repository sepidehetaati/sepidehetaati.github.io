About

Hi, I’m Sepideh Etaati. I recently completed my Master’s degree in Mechatronics Engineering at K. N. Toosi University of Technology, where I also earned my Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering. I was admitted to the Master’s program without an internal entrance exam due to my strong academic performance as an undergraduate.

Throughout my academic journey, I’ve focused on interdisciplinary projects that combine engineering principles with machine learning, human behavior analysis, and biomechanics.
